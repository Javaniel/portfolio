Project Overview

This project involved a complete redesign of the client’s e-commerce platform to improve user experience, increase conversion rates, and modernize the brand’s digital presence.

The key challenges included creating a seamless shopping experience across all devices, implementing an intuitive product discovery system, and designing a streamlined checkout process.

I worked closely with the client’s marketing team to ensure that the new design aligned with their brand strategy while introducing modern design elements that would appeal to their target demographic.

The project featured extensive user research and testing phases, with iterations based on real user feedback. The final implementation resulted in a 55% increase in mobile conversions and a 28% decrease in cart abandonment rates.
